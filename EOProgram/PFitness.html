Benefits of Physical activities

Key Points

  • Physical activity has many immediate and long-term benefits.
  • Physical activity helps you immediately feel better, function better, and sleep better.
  • Adults who sit less and do any amount of moderate- to vigorous-intensity physical activity gain some health benefits.

As you begin your fitness program, keep these tips in mind:

  • Start slowly and build up gradually. Give yourself plenty of time to warm up and cool down with easy walking or gentle stretching.
  • Break things up. You don't have to do all your exercise at one time.
  • Be creative. Maybe you walk, bicycle and row.
  • Listen to your body. If you feel pain, shortness of breath, dizziness or nausea, take a break.
